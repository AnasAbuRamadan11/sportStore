About Our Store

Anas store for selling clothes and sports shoes. We have all the international brands at the best prices. There is delivery to all areas in Gaza, and there is the advantage of returning the product.

History Of Our Store

Anas store was opened in 2020, at its beginning it was an online store only and it sold sportswearonly, and delivery was available and payment was also electronic , a year later in 2021, Anas store was opened on the ground and became selling all sports clothes and shoes in all international brands with also online purchase through the Instagram page, and there is delivery of products in all regions within 24 hours, and there is a feature to return the product if there is a problem upon receipt.
